Martha + Alton's Love Child Waffles


Ingredients

Wet Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 4 tablespoons (1/2 stick) butter, melted

Dry Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Garnish

  • Butter and maple syrup for topping, as desired.

Directions

This recipe combines the deliciousness of Martha Stewart's waffle recipe with the technique and science of Alton Brown's recipe.

  • Prep Time
    30 minutes
  • Total Time
    45 minutes
  • Serves
    4
  1. In a small bowl, whisk your eggs.
    A well-whisked egg incorporates as much air as possible.
    Avoid using plastic, as the risidual oil on the surface interacts with the egg.
  2. If not already liquid, microwave butter in a small bowl for 20-30 seconds or until melted.
    Briskly whisk the egg and butter until it becomes an emulsion.
    e-mul-sion noun
    a fine dispersion of minute droplets of one liquid in another in which it is not soluble or miscible.
  3. Add the milk to your eggs and butter combination and whisk until combined. Set aside.
  4. In a large bowl, mix together your dry ingredients (flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt).
  5. Pour the egg, butter and milk mixture over the dry ingredients and gently mix until there are no large lumps.
    Over-whisking will start to change the flour and make it start to release its gluten, which will make for a less fluffy waffle.
    Small lumps are totally fine and will absorb and be mixed in later in the process.
  6. Allow your batter to rest for at least 20 minutes.
    Resting allows the flour to become saturated and 'bloom', making for a much fluffier and more delicious end result.
  7. Preheat your waffle iron to a high heat and lightly grease.
  8. Pour a generous amount of batter into the center of the waffle iron. It should spread until it's about 1/2 an inch from the edges and close.
    Cook until golden brown and crispy.
  9. Immediately after the waffle is done, spread a small amount of butter over the top.
  10. Serve as soon as possible.
    Garnish as desired.
